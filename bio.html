Strategy In Action ➜

My work centers on untangling complex data environments and orchestrating high-trust ecosystems. I have guided teams through lakehouse migrations on Databricks and Redshift, implemented enterprise MLOps programs, and steered reporting platforms responsible for three trillion dollars in daily transactions.

Technology is only half the equation. The rest is applying an uncommon blend of adaptability and vision to connect disparate insights into compelling direction for executives, product leaders, and practitioners.

"Strategy lives at the intersection of technical excellence and decisive leadership. Every transformation succeeds when the roadmap communicates value at every step."
